# The Candor Project

## White Paper

American law and professional culture have placed deep trust in licensed professions: when a practitioner knowingly makes false claims that result in harm, or even the potential to cause harm, that individual is held accountable and stripped of their professional licenses, preventing them from acting in that capacity ever again. This paper argues that politicians and media figures should be held to those same standards, and engages the substantial First Amendment questions the framework raises.
